School Background

Marian School Mshangano, Songea was established in 2018 with a vision to provide quality education to the children of Songea and surrounding areas. Founded by the Fransiscan Sisters of St. Mary of Angels (FSMA), our school has grown from humble beginnings to become one of the most respected educational institutions in the Ruvuma region.

Our mission is to nurture holistic development in students by providing a balanced education that emphasizes academic excellence, moral values, and social responsibility. We believe in empowering each child to reach their full potential in a supportive and inclusive environment.

Over the years, Marian School has consistently produced outstanding academic results, with our students regularly ranking among the top performers in national examinations. Our dedicated staff, modern facilities, and comprehensive curriculum provide students with the tools they need to succeed in their academic pursuits and beyond.

At Marian School, we offer both pre primary and primary and education with a focus on science, mathematics, languages, and the arts. Our extracurricular program includes sports, music, drama, and various clubs that help students develop their talents and interests outside the classroom.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why Marian School?

Marian School offers a holistic education that balances academic excellence with character development. Our experienced teachers, modern facilities, and comprehensive curriculum ensure students receive quality education that prepares them for higher education and future careers.

Where is Marian School located?

Marian School is located in Mshangano, Songea in the Ruvuma Region of Tanzania. Our campus is situated in a serene environment conducive to learning, approximately 5 kilometers from the Songea town center.

What are the admission requirements?

Admission to Marian School requires birth certicate, passing an entrance examination, and an interview with the school administration. For primary school, children must be at least 2 years old by January of the admission year.

Does the school offer transportation?

Yes, Marian School provides transportation services for students living in Songea and surrounding areas. Our school buses follow designated routes and are equipped with safety features. Transportation fees are separate from tuition and are billed termly.

Does the school provide food?

Yes, Marian School provides high quality and nutritious food to all pupils.
